Who We Are

A women's health initiative evolving a tradition of care into a modern standard of support

The Ọmụgwọ Maternal Welfare Initiative exists to ensure that no woman regardless of their socio-economic status is left to navigate menstruation, pregnancy, postpartum recovery, newborn care, mental wellness, or menopause without informed support.

We combine compassionate community care with evidence-based education so women can move through every reproductive milestone with confidence and dignity.

What We Do

Programs designed to support women through every stage of reproductive life

Maternal Health

We bridge traditional wisdom and evidence-based practice through prenatal and postnatal education that supports safe, holistic preparedness and recovery.

Newborn Care

We equip families with practical, evidence-based guidance so newborn care is approached with preparedness, safety, and confidence.

Cyclical Wellness

We promote health literacy for menstruation, menopause, and the transitions in between so women are supported across their full life reproductive journey.

Mental Health & Wellness

We center emotional wellbeing with resources that recognize maternal mental health as an essential part of complete care.

Our Mission

Bringing informed support beyond clinic walls and into women's daily lives

OMWI was built in response to the gap between what women need and what many healthcare systems in developing countries fail to provide in underserved communities.

  • Maternal and child health education rooted in evidence.
  • Support for menstrual, postpartum, and menopausal transitions.
  • Community-based care that promotes equity and dignity.
