Our Purpose

The Ọmụgwọ Maternal Welfare Initiative is a women's health and wellness organization dedicated to fostering health equity and reproductive dignity through education, facilitating access to healthcare, and providing compassionate support.

Our work is centered on improving maternal and child health, strengthening prenatal and postnatal care, promoting mental wellness, and fostering community-based empowerment for women across every stage of reproductive life.

Our Story

Evolving a tradition of care into a modern standard of women's health equity

The Root of Our Mission

OMWI began with a profound respect for the traditional spirit of Ọmụgwọ, the sacred period of postpartum care and communal support that has sustained mothers for generations.

We recognized that while this tradition is built on deep love and nourishment, the modern world requires an expanded standard of care so every woman can navigate her health journey with safety and dignity.

The Need for Evolution

OMWI was founded after seeing a critical gap in the Nigerian women's healthcare landscape: a lack of health equity and reproductive dignity, alongside poor maternal healthcare conditions contributing to a high maternal mortality rates.

Health for All Women, Without Borders

As our vision expanded, so did our understanding that reproductive dignity does not begin at birth or end after the postpartum period.

  • The beginning: navigating menstrual health and literacy.
  • The transition: supporting safe prenatal, postnatal, and newborn care.
  • The wisdom stage: guiding menopausal wellness with dignity.
  • The constant: prioritizing mental health and wellness as a foundation for all care.

Our Future

OMWI is building a movement to make women's health equity and reproductive dignity a global standard.

By dismantling geographic and socioeconomic barriers, we are fostering a world where every woman is empowered with the knowledge to lead her own reproductive health journey with confidence.

Honoring the past, protecting the present, and evolving the future of women's health.

OMWI exists to ensure that every woman, at every stage of life, is met with informed support, health equity, and reproductive dignity.
